Latin names for animals

2023-09-26

Today, Ian told me several animal names which has Latin roots:

  1. Praying mantis: mantis is the Latin word for “bug”

  2. angler fish: angler is the Latin word for “lantern”

But this turns out to be not right: lanternfish is different. Also, mantis means “prophet”, and angler means “bait”.

When I told him two days later, he said he goth the information from internet. He even messed up prophet with profit. He said daily prophet in Harry Potter. But I stick to “daily profit”. Actually, he is right with the Harry Porter thing: daily prophet is a newspaper!
