Longdivision in Latex

2023-03-19

  • I tried to find a way to write long multiplication/division in Latex and found here very interesting;

  • There is a polynom package, which could even do factorization directly: https://ctan.org/pkg/polynom?lang=en

  • for example:

\(2X^3+X^2-7X+3=\polyfactorize{2X^3+X^2-7X+3}\)

  • fancyhdr – Extensive control of page headers and footers in LATEX2ε

  • longdivision It is amazing!

\[ \longdivision[stage=0]{5.3}{37} \quad \longdivision[stage=1]{5.3}{37} \quad \longdivision[stage=2]{5.3}{37} \quad \longdivision[stage=3]{5.3}{37} \quad \longdivision[stage=4]{5.3}{37} \]

  • It seems like it does not work in html file. For long multiplication, ChatGPT suggests the codes as follows:

\[\begin{align*} &\phantom{0}\,1234 \\ &\times\phantom{0}\,567 \\ \cline{1-2} &\phantom{0}\,7404 \\ &\phantom{0}6170\phantom{0} \\ &4936\phantom{00} \\ \cline{1-2} &\phantom{0}700278 \end{align*}\]
